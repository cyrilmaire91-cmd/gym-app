Session 1 — Quad Dominant

60–70 min Muay Thai S&C
Strength Block
Belt Squat
3 × 5 @ 3 RIR · Rest 3 min · Tempo 3-1-1
Belt weight, vertical tibia, controlled 3s descent. Sub: Hack squat → Leg press.
Hypertrophy Block · Superset · 3–4 sets
A1 / A2
A1
DB Incline Press — Neutral Grip
3 × 8–10 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-1-1
Neutral grip, elbows at 45°, no fixed machines (left shoulder). 2s descent, squeeze at top.
A2
Chest-Supported Row — DB or Machine
3 × 10–12 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-0-1
Chest on pad, elbows back, no trunk rotation. Full control.
Tri-set · Shoulders + Arms · 3 sets
B1 / B2 / B3
B1
Face Pull — Rope
3 × 15 · RIR 1 · Minimal rest
High pulley, pull toward face, elbows high, external rotation at end of movement.
B2
Hammer Curl — Alternating DB
3 × 10 each side · RIR 1
Neutral grip, no swinging. Concentric 1s, eccentric 2s.
B3
Tricep Pushdown — Rope
3 × 12 · RIR 1
Elbows fixed, full extension, slightly leaning forward. Straight wrist (TFCC).
Core · Anti-Rotation
Pallof Press — Cable
3 × 10 each side · RIR 1 · Tempo 2-2-2
Pulley at navel height, arms extended, do not let cable rotate torso. Active bracing.
Cardio Conditioning · Sprint Intervals
Sprints — Assault Bike (default) · Treadmill if knee allows
6 × 20 s effort / 40s rest · Volume adjusted per week
RPE 9 during sprints. Total volume progressive per periodization (see perio bar).

Development

Session 2 — Posterior Chain

60–70 min Muay Thai S&C
Push · Chest
DB Bench Press — Neutral Grip
3 × 8 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-1-1
Neutral grip, elbows at 45°, no fixed machines (left shoulder). Controlled descent.
Strength Block
Trap Bar Deadlift
3 × 5 @ 3 RIR · Rest 3 min · Tempo 3-1-1
Neutral spine, hips back, drive through floor. Trap bar keeps torso upright — no lumbar shear. Stop if lumbar pain >2/10.
Hypertrophy Block · 3–4 sets
Neutral-Grip Pulldown — Close or Neutral Bar
3 × 8–10 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-1-2
Neutral/close grip, pull toward clavicle, elbows toward hips. Full stretch at top. No wide pronated grip (golfer's elbow).
Tri-set · Shoulders + Back · 3 sets
B1 / B2 / B3
B1
Lateral Raise — Cable or DB
3 × 15 · RIR 1 · Minimal rest
Slight forward lean, neutral grip, raise to horizontal. Full control.
B2
Assisted Pull-Up — Machine
3 × 8–10 · RIR 1
Neutral grip only. No supinated grip (TFCC + golfer's elbow). Full extension at bottom, chin above bar.
B3
Tricep Pushdown — Rope
3 × 12 · RIR 1
Elbows fixed at sides, full extension, slight forward lean. Straight wrist. Safer than overhead for golfer's elbow.
Core · Lumbar Stability
Hanging Knee Raise
3 × 12–15 · RIR 1 · Controlled Tempo
No swinging. Knees to chest, lower slowly. Neutral grip if possible (TFCC).
Cardio Conditioning · Bike Intervals
Bike — Aerobic Intervals
20 min · Moderate effort / Volume adjusted per week
RPE 6–7. Maintain steady cadence. Total volume progressive per periodization (see perio bar).

Session 3 — Unilateral

60–70 min Muay Thai S&C
Strength Block · Unilateral
Weighted Bulgarian Split Squat — DB or Smith Machine
3 × 5 each side @ 3 RIR · Rest 3 min · Tempo 3-1-1
Back foot on bench, front tibia vertical. 3s descent, pause at bottom, explosive push. Sub: Unilateral leg press.
Hypertrophy Block · Superset · 3–4 sets
A1 / A2
A1
DB Shoulder Press — Neutral Grip
3 × 10–12 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-1-1
Neutral grip, elbows at 45°, no behind-the-neck press. Left shoulder — watch for pain.
A2
Cable Row — Neutral Grip
3 × 12 · RIR 2 · Tempo 2-0-2
Low pulley, V-bar, pull toward navel, elbows close to body. Full stretch forward.
Bi-set · Isolation · 3 sets
B1 / B2
B1
Lateral Raise — Unilateral Cable
3 × 15 each side · RIR 1 · Minimal rest
Low pulley, arms slightly bent, controlled lateral raise. Full control on descent.
B2
Hammer Curl — Unilateral Cable
3 × 10 each side · RIR 1
Neutral grip via D-handle, no compensation. Eccentric 2s.
Core · Rotation
Pallof Press — Cable
3 × 10 each side · RIR 1 · Tempo 2-2-2
Pulley at navel height, arms fully extended. Do not let cable rotate torso. Active bracing. Sub: Cable Woodchop — High to Low (Ambrose-recommended). Torso rotation, not shoulders. Arms extended, feet stable. Avoid if lumbago is flared.
Cardio Conditioning · Intervals
Intervals — Assault Bike (default) · Treadmill if knee allows
4 × 30 s effort / 60s rest · Volume adjusted per week
RPE 8–9 during efforts. Total volume progressive per periodization (see perio bar).
